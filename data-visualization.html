Data Visualization

Map of Sea Arrivals in Italy

This first graph is a survey about the number of immigrants that arrived by sea in Italy. In each balloon is contained: the number of immigrant that arrive in Italy, where the immigrant came from and the year in which they arrive to Italy.

A little guide to use the filters:

  • In Group By select Year
  • In Shade by, select Country
  • In Size by click on Number of people
  • You can then plot the data as you want, with baloons, maps or barcharts

Distinction between refugee claim, disembarks and the places available in the SPARs

In this chart we can notice that the number of disembarks has increased from 2010 to 2016, then it collapses, after the policies of the two Interior Ministers Marco Minniti and Matteo Salvini. The graph shows that the number of places available in SPRARs still remain constantly very low, in opposition to the refugee claims, while extraordinary places of reception had a proportional increase.

Map of Informal Settlements

This map shows the informal settlements through Italy, making a distinction between the different kind of this particular modality of occupation, providing the number of people that live there and the presence of electricity and water per settlement.
